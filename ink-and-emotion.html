Celeste Ashford
"I read to feel less alone in the world."
Literary Fiction Romance Slow Reads Bookish Tea Marginalia

Hello, dear reader. I'm Celeste — a librarian's daughter, a chronic highlighter, and someone who has cried over fictional characters more times than I can count. Ink & Emotion is my small corner of the internet where I write about books the way they deserve to be written about: honestly, tenderly, and with far too many feelings.

I believe a great book doesn't just tell a story — it becomes part of your story. I write for the readers who dog-ear pages, who finish a novel and need to sit quietly for a while, who recommend books like they're handing someone a piece of their soul.

Every review here is written with care. No star ratings given lightly. No spoilers without warning. Just one reader's honest heart.
